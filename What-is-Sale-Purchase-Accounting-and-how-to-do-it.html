    What is Sale Purchase Accounting and how to do it?
    19 May, 2021
    Managing sale and purchase records is very critical for any business. Sale and purchase records help businesses to understand their business growth and help to submit tax returns on time.

    Managing sale purchase records for small businesses is difficult and there are not many online tools for small businesses that can be used easily.

    Sale purchase accounting is a method where a business records all types of sales in Sale section and all types of purchases in Purchase section.

    Small businesses generally need a simple tool where they can record their daily sale and purchase records. They simply want to manage sale/purchase details, for example - client name, product name, product price, quantity sold, total amount, etc.

    However, almost every software is designed for large businesses that need technical skills and paid. These tools are very easy to learn for a normal business person and can't afford to pay. Bookkeeping app solves this problem. Bookkeeping is designed to small businesses and easy to use for everyone

    Bookkeeping app is simple software which is helpful for sale purchase accounting for small businesses.

    How to add sale purchase entry in Bookkeeping App -
    Adding a new entry in Bookkeeping app is very simple. You need to follow the following steps -
    1. Select a Sale or Purchase button on home page
    2. Enter client name and product name (optional)
    3. Enter product Price and Price Unit. For example - Price of a 250 ml milk packet is 10 rupees, then Price is 10 rupees and Price Unit is 250 ml
    4. Enter quantity of sale/purchase
    5. Total Amount is auto-calculated. Total Amount = Price * Quantity
    6. Enter Cash. If you purchase something, you pay(-) Cash. If you sell something, you receive(+) Cash. Cash can also be zero
    7. Credit is auto-calculated. Credit = Total Amount - Cash Amount. If you purchase something, you will pay(-) the credit. If you sell something, you will receive(+) the credit.
    8. All Sale/Purchase entry are added on home page
