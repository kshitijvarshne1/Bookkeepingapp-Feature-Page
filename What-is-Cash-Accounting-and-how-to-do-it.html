    What is Cash Accounting and how to do it?
    19 May, 2021
    Managing cash is helpful for any business to keep the business sustainable and growing. Cash is important for any business for day-to-day expenses.

    Cash accounting helps to understand the cash flow to any business. Cash accounting is a method where a business records all the cash revenues as well as cash expenses.

    Cash accounting is helpful for small businesses as they do their businesses mostly in cash.

    Generally, small businesses maintain cash records in notebooks as they are not familiar with digital tools. Now, there are some free and easy to use tools available, such as - Bookkeeping App.
    How to do Cash Accounting in Bookkeeping App -
    Cash accounting in Bookkeeping App is very simple. You just need to add Cash Amount for every cash entry.
    Step to follow :
    1. Select "Sale" or "Purchase" button on home page
    2.Enter Client Name and Product Name (optional)
    3. Enter Product Price, Quantity (optional)
    4. Enter Cash Amount
    5. Click on "Save" button to save the entry

    You can simply enter the cash amount and leave everything else blank while adding any cash entry. Or, you can also add other details as well if they are important to record, for example - client name, product name, product price, etc.
